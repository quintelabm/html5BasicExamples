First Recipe

This recipe unfortunately reminds me of my ex but it was the first recipe I made by myself that is really easy done in 30 minutes that tastes amazing! The original recipe is taken from Jamie Oliver book.

List of Ingredients:

  • penne pasta
  • cammembert cheese
  • spinach
  • garlic
  • olive oil
  • rosemary
  • salt and pepper

Steps:

  1. pre-heat oven and put water in a pan to boil.
  2. remove top of cheese and add garlic slices, rosemary, olive oil, salt and pepper.
  3. put the cheese in the oven and leave for 20 minutes.
  4. cook the pasta as recommended and when 1 minute is left to finish cooking add the spinach leaves.
  5. drain pasta.
  6. the cheese is probably ready by now.
  7. serve cheese on top of the pasta as a sauce.
